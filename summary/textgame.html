Save Erica

This is a game about your bestfriend Erica. You have a completely ordinary conversation until someone knocks on the door...
Erica doesn't know how to react and asks for your advice. What will you tell her to do?
Every choice of yours will have an impact on how things will end for Erica.

This game is an interactive game with over 50 different situations and multiple endings, which means that anything could happen...
The game is fully played out as a SMS-conversation between you and your friend, Erica, with you being able to choose different reponses.
It's created in HTML, CSS, JS, AJAX and jQuery.
